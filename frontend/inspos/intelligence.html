Privacy by design

Your captures.
Your machine.
Full stop.

On-device AI is not a marketing claim. It is an architectural constraint we built the product around from day one. There is no pathway — even technically — for your data to leave your device.

01
Zero network calls for processing
The AI model runs locally. Processing never makes an outbound request. Your images are not serialised, compressed, or transmitted anywhere.
02
Local-first storage
All extracted data is stored in an encrypted local database on your machine. You own the database file. You can delete it, export it, or move it.
03
No telemetry, no analytics
We do not track what you capture, what you search for, or what categories you use. Snapwise has no insight into your archive.
04
Open to audit
The local AI model and storage layer are open to independent audit. If you want to verify the claims above, you can inspect the code yourself.
